Meditation Guide

Meditation is a practice that helps you focus your mind, gain a sense of calm, and reduce stress. Follow these steps for an effective meditation session:

  1. Find a quiet space where you won't be disturbed.
  2. Set a timer for your meditation duration.
  3. Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths.
  4. Focus your attention on your breath, noticing the sensations of inhaling and exhaling.
  5. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to your breath.
  6. When your timer goes off, take a moment to notice how you feel before opening your eyes.
